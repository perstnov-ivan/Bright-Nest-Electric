Services

Electrical work, done right.

From a single circuit to a full home rewire — carefully planned, properly permitted, and professionally installed.

01

Electrical Service Upgrades

Upgrade your electrical service from 100A to 200A or 400A. We handle the load calculations, permits, utility coordination, and every detail needed for a safe, code-compliant installation.

02

EV Charger Circuits

Dedicated Level 2 charging circuits, properly sized for your vehicle and your home's electrical system.

03

Standby Generators

Generator and transfer switch installation, properly sized to keep your essential circuits running during power outages.

04

Whole-Home Rewires

Replace outdated wiring with a safe, modern, code-compliant electrical system built for today's electrical demands.

05

ADU & Duplex Electrical

Complete electrical installations for accessory dwelling units, duplexes, and conversions — including subpanels, separate metering, and code-compliant wiring.

06

Smart Home Wiring

Electrical and low-voltage wiring for smart lighting, security cameras, networking, and home automation systems.

07

Outdoor & Pool Circuits

Bonded, GFCI-protected circuits for pools, hot tubs, and outdoor kitchens — installed safely, inspected properly, and built to meet code.

08

Rental & Investor Electrical Services

Pre-purchase inspections and code-compliance upgrades for rental properties and investment homes — including electrical safety improvements and panel replacements.
